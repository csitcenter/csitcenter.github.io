First Semester
The first semester of B.Sc. CSIT program includes core courses in Introduction to Information Technology, C Programming, Digital Logic, Mathematics I (Calculus), and Physics. information technology and related disciplines, including programming, digital circuits, calculus, and physics. 
Introduction to Information Technology

Rs0

Information Technology, covering topics such as introduction, hardware, software, memory, input/output, data representation, database, networks and data communication, Internet, multimedia, and computer security. 

C Programming

Rs0

The course C Programming is focused on the concepts of structured programming using C programming language. Its objective the programming techniques in C.

Digital Logic

Rs0

Boolean algebra and its application in circuit analysis, multilevel gate networks, flip-flops, counters logic devices, synchronous and asynchronous sequential logic, and digital integrated circuits. 

Mathematics I (Calculus)

Rs0

limits, continuity, differentiation, integration of functions of one variable, logarithmic and exponential functions, applications of derivatives, antiderivatives, differential equations, vectors and applications, partial derivatives, and multiple integrals.

Physics

Rs0

fundamentals of physics and computer science and information technology, including oscillations, electromagnetic theory, quantum mechanics, band theory, semiconductors, universal logic gates, and the physics of manufacturing integrated circuits.

KEC Questions Bank

Rs0

Collection of Question like Tu Question, Pu Questions Model Questions and Terminal Questions and kind of VVI Questions For B.Sc Csit Whole Semester Questions.
